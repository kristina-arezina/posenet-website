Human pose estimation is one of the main puzzles in computer vision that has been studied for over 15 years!

Pose estimation refers to computer vision techniques that detect human shapes in images and videos, so that one could determine, for instance, where someoneâ€™s knee is in an image or video. Pose estimation technology is not recognizing who / what is in an image like object detection does. Instead, the algorithm is simply estimating where key body joints are. Human Pose Estimation has some pretty cool applications in medicine, health and fitness, and entertainment industries.
